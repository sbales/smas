Auditions

Are you new to the Springfield area and want to join? Currently Audition season is closed but private, mid-season, auditions can be made by contacting the Artistic Director, Tom Matrone, directly. Click here to send him an email.
Auditions and acceptance into Springfield Mid-America Singers is based on tone quality, tonal memory, and overall musical phrasing. Although not always required, we do expect our members to be between 18 and 65 years of age. The SMAS membership fee is $50 and this includes printing of music. There is also required concert attire that will be your responsibility to pay for. For men this includes purchase of a tuxedo and for women this includes the purchase and alterations of a dress.Our rehearsal season starts on the first Tuesday after Labor Day and contiunes until the last Sunday in April. We have one Tuesday evening rehearsal for 1.5 hours each week. There are four concerts per year.
